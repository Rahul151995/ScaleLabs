About Greendeck

Company


Founded in 2017, Greendeck helps brands and retailers with the right competitive intelligence they need to drive speed, efficiency and better decision making. All this leads to higher revenue, better margins and an improved customer experience.

We are a proud Techstars Company and excited to be part of the wonderful network of mentors and investors from Techstars, SAP.iO, Startupbootcamp and Y Combinator Startup School.
